My Academic Projects and Activities

Here you can find the main projects and activities that I have been involved in during my academic career.

December, 2021

M.Sc. in Data Science and Engineering: Last year, on December 20, I successfully defended my master thesis titled "A Cluster-Based Trip Prediction Graph Neural Network Model for Bike Sharing Systems", with a grade of 19 out of 20. I worked under the supervision of Professors Cláudia Soares and Manuel Marques, and our work concerns a bicycle trips predictor based on Graph Neural Network embeddings in the context of Bike Sharing Systems.

First Paper Submitted! Scientific Paper Submission to the journal IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems of the work developed with my supervisors during my master thesis.

You can check our paper here and click here for more details of our work.

February, 2021

Collaboration with Alfredo AI: In the first semester in the last year of the master's degree, in the context of the discipline Applications of Data Science and Engineering at IST, I developed with a colleague the project "Automatic Generation of Real Estate Listing Text Descriptions for Residential Real Estate Using a Transformer" in the field of Natural Language Processing. This project emerged as an improvement of an old prototype of Real Estate Machine Learning Startup, Alfredo AI, which through some features of the property creates the respective real estate listing using Machine Learning techniques.

You can check here our work.

July, 2019

I completed my Bachelor's studies! In summer 2019, I completed my Bachelor's studies at FCUL in Applied Mathematics - Branch of Statistics and Operational Research.

May 22, 2019

Tec Labs Open Day: I and my team presented our business idea developed in the Entrepreneurship classes at FCUL, in the scope of Road Safety - Our idea consisted of a system for screening users, restricting the use of Bike Sharing Systems by non-sober individuals.

July 13, 2018

Monitor at ULisboa: Activities' Monitor in the Summer program at ULisboa in the activities of the Department of Statistics and Operational Research at FCUL.
